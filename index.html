The Academy Color Encoding System (ACES)a> is a free, open, device-independent color management and image interchange system used in the production of movies, series or games. ACES adoption has increased during the last decade and the system is becoming a successful standard in the Media and Entertainment Industry. However, amid of the growing success, ACES users are frequently reporting defects occurring on their imagery while using the system.
